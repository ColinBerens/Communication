My first gamejam: Slother

Are you mad too when humans destroy our nature? If so slother is the perfect for you. In this game you play as the sloth king who commands the other animals in the forest. You need them to save your tree from the evil lumbers. This fun tower defense game gives you new a way to think how to place your turrets
The game is available on ITCH.io.
The keyfeature is that you can design your own map. You can place obstacles that change the path of the lumbers.
